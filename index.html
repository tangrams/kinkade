This is a spheremap painter. Draw in the box on the right to paint a spheremap, which will color the interactive map on the left.

Spheremaps are images used to color 3D geometry, as though they were wrapped around a sphere. Colors on the left side of the image will be the color of 3D faces which point left.

Try the example spheremaps at various sample locations.

The maps are drawn using the Tangram map library. Sign in with your Mapzen account to save scenes, and then open and edit them in Tangram Play.

To read about how we use spheremaps in our Walkabout basemap, read our blog post Sphere Maps.

To learn more about using spheremaps in cartography, read out blog post Mapping Mountains.
